Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

BibSure is operated by Kavih AI Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use BibSure at bibsure.vercel.app.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

BibSure is a client-side application. Your files (.bib, .ris, .pdf) are processed entirely in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers. We have no access to the content of your bibliography files or research papers.

2. Data We Collect

  • Analytics: We use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous page view data including pages visited, country, device type, and browser. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • External API calls: To validate citations, your browser makes direct requests to the CrossRef REST API (crossref.org). These requests include citation metadata (title, author, DOI) but not your identity. CrossRef's privacy policy applies to these requests.

3. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Vercel Analytics uses privacy-preserving techniques and does not require cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

  • CrossRef API — citation validation database (crossref.org/privacy)
  • Vercel — hosting and analytics (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)

5. Children's Privacy

BibSure is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

Questions? Email us at contact@kavihai.com.
